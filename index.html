About Me

I'm Nikola, a cybersecurity specialist and an ethical hacker. Relentless passion for tech, living for challenges, and never being content with mediocrity are the things that drive me to master whatever is in my sight. Curiosity and the urge to understand how everything works are driving forces in my life. My playgrounds are offensive security, scripting, and automation. My journey into hacking began with simple curiosity, which then quickly transformed into a passion that has defined my modern-day self. I have hands-on experience in both Red Teaming and Blue Teaming, meaning I have experience in the detection of vulnerabilities and defending against them. My toolkit is varied, including tools within Kali Linux, Flipper Zero, and portable setups like Kali NetHunter.

I've even taken on the programming of industrial robots. If it can be hacked, I'm there.

Hacking is just part of the whole. My mindset is that of a polymath, where I not only continue to build software but also leverage my expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, Python, and Bash, especially for scripting purposes, and reverse engineering alongside the intricacies of human behavior, such as microexpressions. To know how people think helps me be an excellent social engineer; it is all part of leveling up who I am in general, both as a professional and a person.

When I’m not in front of my screen, you can find me at cyber events, local or international, sharing my knowledge, gaining new skills, and connecting with the community. I'm also working on founding the first hackerspace in Kragujevac—a community-driven environment for tech enthusiasts.

I’m always hungry to learn more—whether it's tackling Bug Bounties, hunting zero-days, or creating compact and portable hacking tools. My goal is to keep leveling up my skills so I can be the person standing between malicious actors and their targets, making the digital world a little safer, one vulnerability at a time. My handle, "v1nd1c7," might sound intimidating, but at its core, it’s about respect. Ultimately, I’m all about community, innovation, and using my skills for good. Welcome to my journey—an endless pursuit of knowledge, passion, and relentless growth.
